AFRICA

Animal- Cheetah

  • As well as having spots on their fur, cheetahs have spots on their skin!
  • The cheetah is the world's fasted land animal- they can run 70mph!
  • The paws on cheetah's feet are super hard like rubber on a tire.

Animal- African Elephant

  • Elephants are the world's largest land mammal- weighing at 6000kg!
  • They ears have a large surface area which helps them radiate heat from the sun
  • Elephants can hear a large range of sounds- 10 octives!

Animal- Zebra

  • In Africa there are three different species of zebra- the plains zebra, Grevy's zebra and mountain zebra.
  • Within a herd, zebras tend to stay together in smaller family groups.
  • Zebras are constantly on the move for fresh grass to eat- they'll travel thousands of kilometres in search of grass
