Animal- Norwegian Lemming

  • The Norwegian lemming is a mouse-like rodent endemic to Norway, western and northern Sweden, northern Finland, and the Kola Peninsula of Russia, according to the International Union for the Conservation of Nature.
  • It weighs from 20 to 130 grams, and the length ranges from 8 to 17.5 centimeters.
  • Norwegian lemmings' fur colors are black and brown, with golden yellow strips. Its underbelly is lighter than the rest of the fur.
