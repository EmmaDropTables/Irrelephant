ASIA

Animal- Panda

  • All the pandas in the world are on loan from China.
  • More than 60% of pandas born in captivity die in a week.
  • 99% of a pandas diet is bamboo!

Animal- Malayan tapir

  • This animal whistles to communicate!
  • They have very poor eyesight so they rely on their ears and sense on smell.
  • Malayan tapirs prefer to live near water so they can swim and drink.

Animal- Red Panda

  • Red pandas live in the mountains of Nepal, central China and northern Myanmar.
  • They have tear tracks from their eyes to the corner of their mouths. These markings help keep the sun out of their eyes
  • Their tail helps them maintain their balance as they climb trees.
