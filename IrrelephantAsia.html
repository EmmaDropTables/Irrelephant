Animal- Panda

  • All the pandas in the world are on loan from China.
  • More than 60% of pandas born in captivity die in a week.
  • 99% of a pandas diet is bamboo!

Animal- Malayan tapir

  • This animal whistles to communicate!
  • They have very poor eyesite so they rely on their ears and sense on smell.
  • Malayan tapirs prefer to live near water so they can swim and drink.

Animal- Zebra

  • In Africa there are three different species of zebra- the plains zebra, Grevy's zebra and mountain zebra.
  • Within a herd, zebras tend to stay together in smaller family groups.
  • Zebras are constantly on the move for fresh grass to eat- they'll travel thousands of kilometres in search of grass
